> Agriculture system
# The land in a valley bathed in sunlight was brought to life by the rhythm of agriculture. Planting seeds that whispered promises of harvests, generations of farmers arose with the morning. To maintain the vitality of the ground, they tended to the soil by engaging in companion planting and crop rotation. Golden grains and vivid vegetables grew in the fields, and fragrant fruits blossomed in the fruit trees.

Communities came together to enjoy a wealth of food as the seasons changed, sharing not only food but also ages-old tales. By embracing sustainable practices and learning to live with the moods of nature, they protected their house.
But obstacles were in their way; their lives were under danger from urbanization and climate change. A resilient future where agriculture thrived in harmony with the soil, feeding both people and planet, was fostered by creative minds that emerged in response, fusing tradition with technology.
