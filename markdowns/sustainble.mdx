# A little village flourished in a sun-drenched valley, its energy blended with the soil. They suffered by increased expenses and pollution while they were dependent on fossil fuels. Meeting to discuss renewable energy, the town's residents were motivated by a vision of a cleaner future.

The spirit of cooperation blossomed and the air became clearer.
