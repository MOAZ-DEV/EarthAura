# Water resources

A small settlement was supported by a river that meandered through woodlands and fields in a lovely valley. Its clean waterways were essential for farming, fishing, and drinking for many generations. However, as the population increased, the river's banks began to erode due to increased demand. 
When a terrible drought hit one summer, the villagers got together and began to worry about their future. Motivated by need, they developed treatments, including rainwater collection, effective irrigation, and community gardens. 
Together, they fixed old wells and planted trees to preserve the soil. The people learnt to treasure and safeguard this priceless resource as the river gradually started to heal. They adopted care and an increased respect for water, making sure that next generations would live in balance with the vital resource of the natural world.
